# Socratic - What It Does (Simple Script)

**"Socratic is an intelligent personal tutor that lives in your browser."**

## What it actually does:
1.  **It Teaches You, It Doesn't Just Cheat:**
    "Unlike basic chatbots that just give you the answer, Socratic acts like a real teacher. It guides you to the solution step-by-step so you actually understand the topic."

2.  **You Can Talk to It:**
    "You don't just have to type. You can have a real voice conversation with it, asking questions out loud just like you would in a classroom."

3.  **It Can "See":**
    "If you're stuck on a textbook diagram or a problem, you can just upload a photo of it. The AI analyzes the image and explains it to you instantly."

4.  **It Remembers You:**
    "It tracks everything you learn and saves your progress, so you can review your lessons or download them to study later."

**Summary:**
"Basically, it's a private tutor that's available 24/7 to help you master any subject through conversation, voice, and visuals."
